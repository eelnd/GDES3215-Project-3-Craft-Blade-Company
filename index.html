frequently asked questions

how do i recycle for credit?

easy! each of our products come with small closable containers made from recycled plastic bottles. you put your old blades in there and send them back for credit. you get $1.00 back for every 5 blades recycled. the blades you recycle get reused to make new blades instead of going to waste.

how much is shipping?

on orders $15 or more (without tax) shipping is free. we offer standard, express, and international shipping (shipping prices may vary depending on carrier and weight).

how fast is shipping?

standard shipping usually takes 4-7 business days, express usually takes 1-2 business days, and international usually takes 7-10 business days. we ship our orders out within 48 hours of you receiving your confirmation email. shipping times may differ based on weather, but we will let you know if your package is delayed. all packages come with tracking numbers so you can also track your package.

our story

the craft blade company started as an idea between two friends who used craft knives all the time for projects such as model making and paper crafting. since the blades do not have the longest life span depending on how often they are used they wanted a way to still be able to use them but in a not as harmful way. they decided to produce the different blades from recycled scrap metal and they also have a turn it in program where people can send dull blades back for credit on their site to buy new ones.
two years later we have been working hard on making them affordable, good quality, durable, and greener. now we have been making them since 1998.
